Our Solar System

Explore the fascinating planets that make up our cosmic neighborhood

Mercury

The smallest planet in our solar system and closest to the Sun.

Diameter: 4,880 km

Orbit Period: 88 Earth days

Surface Temp: -173°C to 427°C

Venus

The hottest planet with a toxic atmosphere of carbon dioxide.

Diameter: 12,104 km

Orbit Period: 225 Earth days

Surface Temp: 462°C

Earth

Our home planet and the only known place in the universe with life.

Diameter: 12,742 km

Orbit Period: 365.25 days

Surface Temp: -88°C to 58°C

Mars

The Red Planet with the largest dust storms in the solar system.

Diameter: 6,779 km

Orbit Period: 687 Earth days

Surface Temp: -153°C to 20°C

Jupiter

The largest planet with a Great Red Spot storm for centuries.

Diameter: 139,820 km

Orbit Period: 4,333 Earth days

Surface Temp: -108°C

Saturn

Famous for its beautiful rings made of ice and rock.

Diameter: 116,460 km

Orbit Period: 10,756 Earth days

Surface Temp: -139°C

About Galactic Explorer

Galactic Explorer is an interactive platform designed to bring the wonders of our solar system to your fingertips. With stunning visuals and accurate scientific data, we aim to inspire the next generation of space explorers.

Our mission is to make space education accessible and engaging for everyone, from curious students to astronomy enthusiasts.
